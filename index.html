The Vision

Quiet luxury, engineered for living.

Nomad Towers is a landmark residential development moments from the Westlands business district. Every residence is oriented to the light — floor-to-ceiling glazing, generous terraces and interiors finished in natural stone, oak and brushed brass.

Designed around the rhythms of modern Nairobi, the tower pairs serene private homes with two floors of shared amenity — so the city is always close, and never intrusive.
